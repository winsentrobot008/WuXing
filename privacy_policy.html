Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We do not store your real birth date or time on our servers. All calculations are performed locally in your browser using the Lunar-JS library. If you create an account, we only store your email and encrypted Ganzhi (Four Pillars) data for history syncing.

2. Data Security

Your data is processed with encryption. We do not share your personal information or calculation results with any third parties.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Since most data is stored in your local browser storage (localStorage), you can also clear your data by clearing your browser cache.

4. Third-Party Services

We use standard CDN services to load libraries. We do not use trackers or invasive analytics.
